Griffin Menu Studio 1.0.1 — Your real menus, smarter updates

This update loads The Griffin's actual menus into the app and makes updating effortless.

## What's new

- **Your real menus, ready to edit.** All of The Griffin's menus — the set lunches, dinner, lunch, Sunday menus, sharing, buffet, canapés, children's, and more — are now in your **Documents › Griffin Menu Studio › Menus** folder as individual files, ready to open and edit.
- **A place for everything.** Menus, templates and exports each have a home in the Griffin Menu Studio folder, and a one-click **"Open the Griffin Menu Studio folder"** button in Settings takes you straight there. Exports now save to an Exports folder.
- **Effortless updates.** Griffin now updates itself quietly in the background and simply lets you know when a new version is ready — with **Update Now**, **Later** (installs next time you open the app), and the full change log right inside Settings. You can check for updates any time.
- **Redesigned Settings.** A cleaner, fuller Settings page: new-menu defaults, a **Typography** panel to set the coordinated look (font set, size, density, and per-role styling previewed with your own menu content), backup & recovery options, and your real folder locations.
- **Moveable subtitles.** Subtitles are no longer stuck under the title — drag them anywhere between sections and lines, add as many as you like, and remove them with a click.
- **The Print button works.** The Print button on the Export screen now opens your system print dialog.

## Fixes & polish
- Print, export folders, dietary-key handling and dozens of editor refinements.

Thank you for using Griffin Menu Studio.
